Mould Making

Custom tool creation for precision manufacturing

What is Mould Making?

Mould Making is the process of creating a mold (tool) used to produce plastic or resin parts. At VCAN 3D, moulds are made based on the customer’s CAD design.

🔧 The mold can be:

  • Silicone mold (for Vacuum Casting)
  • Metal mold (for Injection Molding)
  • Prototype tooling for low-volume production

Custom CAD-based design
