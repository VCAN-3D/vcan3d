What is FDM 3D Printing?

FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling) is a 3D printing technology that melts thermoplastic filament and deposits it layer by layer to build a part. It is one of the most commonly used and cost-effective 3D printing methods, widely used in Chennai and globally for functional prototypes and engineering components.

🔧 How FDM Works:

  • Plastic filament (PLA, ABS, PETG, Nylon, etc.) is fed into the printer
  • Nozzle heats and melts the filament
  • Melted material is extruded layer by layer
  • Layers cool and solidify to form the final part

⭐ Advantages of FDM 3D Printing

Cost-effective production
Strong & functional parts
Large size printing possible
Suitable for engineering applications
Fast prototype production
