Low Volume Production

Manufacturing from 10 to 1000 pieces with efficiency in Chennai

What is Low Volume Production?

Low Volume Production means manufacturing a small quantity of parts — usually from 10 to 1000 pieces — instead of mass production. VCAN 3D's low volume production services in Chennai are ideal for companies that need limited quantities before moving to full-scale injection molding, helping reduce initial investment and time to market.

🔧 How VCAN 3D Does Low Volume Production:

  • Vacuum Casting (silicone molds)
  • 3D Printing (SLS, MJF, SLA, FDM, DLP)
  • Prototype tooling
  • Short-run Injection Molding

⭐ Benefits of Low Volume Production

Faster time to market
Lower initial investment
Bridge between prototyping & mass production
Design flexibility
