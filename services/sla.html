What is SLA 3D Printing?

SLA (Stereolithography) is a resin-based 3D printing technology that uses a UV laser to cure (harden) liquid photopolymer resin layer by layer to create highly detailed parts. VCAN 3D's SLA services in Chennai deliver exceptional accuracy and surface quality for prototypes and precision components.

🔧 How SLA Works:

  • Liquid resin is filled in a tank
  • UV laser scans the design layer by layer
  • Resin hardens where the laser hits
  • Platform moves layer by layer
  • Final part is cleaned & UV cured

⭐ Advantages of SLA 3D Printing

Very high accuracy
Smooth surface finish
Great for detailed prototypes
Ideal for small & precision parts
