SLA 3D Printing

High-precision resin-based manufacturing

What is SLA?

SLA (Stereolithography) is a resin-based 3D printing technology that uses a laser to cure (harden) liquid photopolymer resin layer by layer to create highly detailed parts.

🔧 How SLA Works:

  • Liquid resin is filled in a tank
  • UV laser scans the design layer
  • Resin hardens where laser hits
  • Platform moves layer by layer
  • Final part is cleaned & UV cured

⭐ Advantages of SLA

Very high accuracy
Smooth surface finish
Good for detailed prototypes
Ideal for small & precision parts
